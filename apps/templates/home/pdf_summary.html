COVID-19 information

Before leaving Israel:

1. The policy in the destination country should be clarified It is your responsibility to find out what the border policy and requirements of the state of the destination are related to the entry of citizens, whether there is a requirement to fill out forms, perform tests, isolation obligation and more. The countries that sell Israel's corona certificates.

2. A PCR test must be performed or 72 hours before departure from Israel Only those who get a negative test result get on the flight. This directive is binding on all those leaving Israel, at any age. The result must be written in English with the passport number of the passenger. No speed tests or tests of any other type will be accepted.

During the flight:

Wear a mask over the nose and mouth (from age 6 and up), keep a distance and maintain personal hygiene.

Before the flight must be presented to the airline representatives:

Confirmation of filling out the form of declaration of departure from Israel. If you have been in Israel for more than 72 hours - one of the following documents: Negative result on the PCR test you did during the 72 hours before the flight. A valid green label produced by the Ministry of Health on the basis of vaccination or recovery. Please note: The entrance to the passenger terminal in Israel is for pilots only. Accompaniment of escorts is not allowed in the terminal, except for escorts of minors or a person with a disability.
